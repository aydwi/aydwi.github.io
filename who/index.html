I’m a computer programmer, and usually go by aydwi on the Internet. I respect lean and assiduously built software and the people who make it possible. Among other things, I’m interested in software (particularly open-source), mathematics and climbing.

These days, I spend my time writing low-level software to find bugs in CPU cores implementing the open-source RISC-V ISA with the fine folks over at Valtrix Systems. Previously, I have collaborated with the team at Freedom of the Press Foundation on the SecureDrop project.

I use GitHub as a platform to publish my open-source projects. Reach out to me via email or Keybase.
